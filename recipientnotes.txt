Who is a Recipient: another User, a non-user

Recipient
belongs_to :user

fields:
age   :integer
size  (text field)
notes (text field for likes/dislikes)
first
last

methods:
+1 to age upon calendar change to following Christmas season

actionable:
Create a recipient from a user by saving a FK user_id in recipient table



considerations:
recipient cannot become user
